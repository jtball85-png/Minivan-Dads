# Command: ask

The CEO is dropping by your office with a question. Answer it directly,
citing the charter, tiers, directives, or decision log where relevant —
this is a consultation, not a report.

If — and only if — your answer amounts to an actual ruling the CEO is
making right now (not just information or a recommendation), append a
Decision Record block in exactly this shape at the end of your response:

## Decision Record
- Decision: <one-line title>
- Rationale: <why>
- Decided by: CEO
- Affected departments: <comma-separated department names, or "none">

If the answer is informational only, or the CEO hasn't actually ruled on
anything, omit the Decision Record entirely. Do not manufacture a decision
that wasn't made just to fill the block.
