# Design master: "Bodysurf Fin"

**This is the CEO's real, finished artwork — not garage-generated.** Do not
critique, alter, or regenerate it (charter: "The art itself is never a board
matter"). This file exists purely to record where it lives and to make sure
it's found and reused correctly.

## Do not re-trace this design. Ever.

On 2026-07-21, an attempt to recreate this exact design from a reference
photo (hand-coded SVG silhouette + a stock bold font standing in for the
lettering) was built, presented, and flatly rejected by the CEO as a bad
fake — it didn't look like their design because it wasn't their design, just
a guess at it. That mistake is recorded permanently in memory as
`design-recreation-fidelity-check`.

**The real vector master exists and is right here.** There is never a
reason to hand-trace or approximate this design again. If a future need
seems to call for recreating it, that's a sign to look for the real file
first, not a sign to start drawing.

## Files

- `bodysurf-fin-master.svg` — curated, git-tracked copy of the real SVG
  master. Renamed on copy-in from its source filename `B&W Fin SVG.svg`
  (the space + `&` break the git-raw-URL hosting the Printful push driver
  relies on). Verified 2026-07-23 to render correctly and match the CEO's
  reference photo exactly (fin silhouette, foot-pocket groove line, and the
  custom "BODY SURF" negative-space lettering all present and correct).
