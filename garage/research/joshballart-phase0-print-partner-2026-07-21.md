hand-touched/local work; theprintspace/creativehub is the runner-up.**
Confidence: high on capabilities (vendor docs + independent reviews), medium on
exact costs (need account-level quotes — next step is samples).

+20% fee and UK-centric ops vs Prodigi's US labs and 24–48h. (3) FinerWorks =
credible US fallback if Prodigi samples disappoint.

**Decision gate (CEO, money): order the same 2 test images as samples from

artist eyes before committing the catalog.**

## Print sizes + borders — research answer

Offer three sizes: **8x10, 11x14, 16x20** ("solve 90% of art display needs" per
multiple sellers/printers; all fit ready-made frames/mats). Optional top end
18x24 or 20x30 later for statement pieces. Border convention: print with a
white border so image + border hits the standard mat/frame sizes (e.g. 8x10
image on 11x14 sheet; 11x14 image on 16x20 sheet); white-on-white matting is
the fine-art default. Ratio caution: 8x10 (4:5) vs 11x14 vs 16x20 (4:5) crop
differently — file prep must derive per-size crops, not one master crop.
