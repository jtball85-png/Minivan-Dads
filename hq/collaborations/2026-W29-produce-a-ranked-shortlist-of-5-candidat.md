## Top pick: **Minivan Dads** (incumbent), hold

It's not just highest on voice fit — it's the only one of the five that *doesn't require a joke-explaining beat* to work as a hat-first badge, which is the whole premise of Pillar 1. "Swagger Wagon" is the strongest runner-up on pure tone but carries real ownership/legal risk (Market Intel's flag stands, and I'd add: it's not *our* joke to claim). "The Sliding Door Society" is the most interesting long-term extension — worth keeping as a sub-line or collection name (e.g. a badge tier) rather than a rename candidate.

**Recommendation to CEO via COO:** don't spend rename energy chasing #2–5 as *replacements* — none clearly outperforms the incumbent on badge-first fit, and Pillar 5 (Focus) argues against reopening the name question without a forcing reason. If the naming question was reopened because of the unidentified third-party .com registration on Minivan Dads, that's a trademark-clearance problem to solve (legal/CEO), not a brand-voice problem to solve by renaming.
