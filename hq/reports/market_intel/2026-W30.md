**3. Permits and insurance groundwork for outdoor workshops — concrete numbers now on record for a CEO/counsel decision.** Ventura County Parks charges permits for professional photography at $190 per day, per location, and photography for multi-day, multi-location, or with props will require a special use permit and a certificate of insurance naming the County as an additional insured. The City of Ventura's film/photo permit process similarly requires a Certificate of Insurance and endorsement page naming the City of Ventura as an additional insured... prior to the release of any commercial film and photography permits, filed a minimum of 30 City business days before your proposed film/photography event. On insurance cost: short-term general liability event coverage runs $49/event for 1–3 days, $99/event for 7 days, $149/event for 90 days or annual plans from about $24.25 per month (about $291 annually), and professional liability (needed specifically for teaching/instruction claims, which general liability does not cover) adds roughly $10.42/month on top of a standard policy — general liability typically does not cover teaching workshops; instruction-related claims need professional liability instead. This is research only, not a legality conclusion — but it's now enough for the CEO to size the real dollar cost of running a permitted beach/park popup.
