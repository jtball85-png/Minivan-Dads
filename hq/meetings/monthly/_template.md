# Monthly Review — {YYYY}-{MM}

A deeper session than the weekly board meeting. Covers strategy, not
operations.

## Trendlines

What the last month of decisions, reports, and (once there is revenue)
finance data show. Grounded in `hq/decisions/log.md` and department reports,
not vibes.

## What we killed and why

Ideas, designs, or directions dropped this month, with rationale. This is
as important a record as what shipped.

## Tier promotion proposals

Any department proposed for a tier change, with the decision-log evidence
supporting it. Tier changes are always decisions — they get logged
separately in `hq/decisions/log.md`, not just noted here.

## Roadmap check-in

Are we on schedule against `hq/charter/roadmap.md`? Which phase are we in,
and what's the exit criteria status?

## Open questions for the CEO

Anything that needs a strategic call, as opposed to an operational one.
